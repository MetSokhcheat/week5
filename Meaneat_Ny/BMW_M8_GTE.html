About the BMW M8 GTE

The BMW M8 GTE represents the pinnacle of motorsport engineering. As BMW’s ultimate endurance race car, it combines cutting-edge aerodynamics, a lightweight build, and a high-performance powertrain to dominate the track.

Key Features

Advanced Aerodynamics

The M8 GTE is engineered for maximum downforce and minimal drag, ensuring stability at high speeds.

Powerful V8 Engine

Its twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers over 500 horsepower, tailored for endurance racing performance.

Racing Heritage

The M8 GTE has competed in legendary events like the 24 Hours of Le Mans, embodying BMW's motorsport legacy.
