About the BMW M4 GT3

The BMW M4 GT3 is a race car built to compete at the highest levels of motorsport. With a commitment to precision engineering and superior performance, the M4 GT3 is designed for endurance racing and GT racing competitions. Powered by a turbocharged inline-six engine and a lightweight chassis, this car delivers exceptional handling and speed, making it a true contender in every race. Since its debut, the M4 GT3 has competed in prestigious series such as the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship and the 24 Hours of Nürburgring. Designed for both speed and durability, it showcases BMW’s dedication to pushing the limits of technology and performance on the race track. The car is equipped with advanced aerodynamics and a sophisticated suspension system that ensures stability and responsiveness even under the most challenging racing conditions. The BMW M4 GT3 is a symbol of excellence in both engineering and racing heritage. Engine Specifications:

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6
  • Output: 500+ hp
  • Torque: 600+ Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed sequential gearbox
  • Weight: Approx. 1,300 kg
  • Aerodynamics: Active aero system for downforce control
  • Chassis: Lightweight carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP)
