About the BMW M2 CS

The BMW M2 CS is the ultimate expression of BMW's commitment to combining agility, power, and luxury. Designed for the thrill-seeker, it delivers track-level performance with daily drivability in a compact coupe design.

Key Features

High-Performance Engine

Equipped with a twin-turbocharged inline-six engine, the M2 CS delivers 444 horsepower, making every drive exhilarating.

Lightweight Design

With carbon fiber-reinforced components, the M2 CS achieves optimal weight distribution and improved handling.

Precision Engineering

From the Adaptive M Suspension to M Compound Brakes, the M2 CS is engineered for precision on and off the track.
