About the BMW M2 CS

The BMW M2 CS is the high-performance variant of the BMW M2, designed to offer the ultimate driving experience for enthusiasts who crave raw power, agility, and precision. The M2 CS takes the M2's aggressive styling and performance to the next level, with a more powerful engine, enhanced suspension, and reduced weight to deliver exceptional handling and speed. Powered by a 3.0L Twin-Turbo inline-6 engine, the M2 CS produces an impressive 444 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, making it one of the most potent models in the M2 lineup. This power is harnessed by an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT), allowing the M2 CS to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.8 seconds. The M2 CS also features a lightweight carbon fiber roof, larger brakes, and an adaptive suspension system that ensures perfect handling on both the road and track. With the precision-tuned M chassis and aggressive styling, the M2 CS delivers a thrilling driving experience whether you're carving through mountain roads or pushing it to the limit on a racetrack. Engine Specifications:

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6
  • Output: 444 hp
  • Torque: 406 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT)
  • 0-60 mph: 3.8 seconds
  • Top Speed: 174 mph
  • Weight: Approx. 1,575 kg
  • Aerodynamics: Aggressive front and rear styling for optimized airflow
